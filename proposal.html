Executive Summary

Slate Health deploys AI agents that replace healthcare administrators — the fastest-growing, least productive cost center in American medicine.
Core Thesis: Healthcare managers have grown 4,800% since 1970 while physicians grew just 200%. The US spends $496 billion per year on billing and insurance administration alone — more than the GDP of most countries. AI can now automate 70%+ of these tasks. Slate captures this opportunity by charging 15% of the labor it replaces, creating a $22B+ revenue TAM with zero-risk adoption for buyers.

What We're Building

Slate Health is an AI-native platform that deploys six specialized agents to handle the core functions of healthcare administration: prior authorization, claims & billing, scheduling, eligibility verification, credentialing, and compliance reporting. Each agent replaces an entire department. They run 24/7, process thousands of transactions per hour, and improve continuously.

Business Model

Performance-based pricing: Slate charges 15% of the admin labor it replaces. Health systems keep 85% of the savings. If Slate doesn't deliver measurable savings, clients pay nothing. This eliminates adoption risk and aligns incentives directly with outcomes.

Market Timing

AI Capability Inflection: Healthcare AI market is $37B in 2025, projected $500B+ by 2034 (37% CAGR). AI now handles 70%+ of revenue cycle tasks autonomously. 54% of all digital health VC funding in 2025 went to AI companies.

Regulatory Tailwinds: CMS electronic prior authorization mandates take effect 2027. Hospital admin expenses doubled from $367B (2011) to $687B (2023). Health systems are desperate for cost reduction.

Each Slate agent replaces an entire administrative department. They operate 24/7, process thousands of transactions per hour, and compound in accuracy daily.

Prior Authorization Agent

Claims & Billing Agent

Why Performance-Based Wins

1. Zero-risk adoption. CFOs don't need to justify upfront spend. If it doesn't work, they pay nothing. This compresses sales cycles from 12-18 months to 3-6 months.

2. Higher ACV over time. As agents automate more tasks, the labor-replaced increases, and so does revenue — without upselling.

3. Sticky by design. Once agents are processing claims and prior auths, switching costs are high. Net revenue retention should exceed 130%.

4. Land and expand. Start with prior auth + billing (50% of admin time), expand to all six agents over 12 months.

Sales Motion

Lead with the ROI calculator. The homepage calculator is the top-of-funnel. CFOs input their numbers, see 5-7x ROI instantly. Converts to a "custom ROI report" lead magnet.

Sell to the CFO, not IT. This is a cost-reduction play, not a technology play. The buyer is the person who signs checks for admin staff — the CFO or COO.

Performance guarantee closes deals. "$0 if no savings" eliminates the procurement objection. Legal and compliance review is faster when there's no financial commitment upfront.

Expand automatically. Once prior auth + billing agents are live, scheduling and eligibility agents deploy naturally. Revenue grows without upsell effort.
